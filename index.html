Hi, I'm Tristan. Welcome to my portfolio!

I am currently wrapping up my third year of Communication and Multimedia Design at the HvA. Over the years, I have experienced a profound change of interest. While well capable of UX/UI design, web design and graphic design, I have developed a knack for audio visual storytelling and creative writing - skills usually not associated with CMD.

I value craft. I bear a healthy dose of perfectionism and a strong desire for authenticity. Naturally, I dislike artificial intelligence - generative AI in particular. I have learned this to be a quick turn off for many employers, but I stand by my principles and will not compromise on my integrity. I hope to find an internship where my values are shared.

Thank you sincerely for taking the time to check out my portfolio - built with love, from scratch :)
