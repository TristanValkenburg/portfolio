ABOUT

Hi, I'’'m Tristan Valkenburg – an actor, model, and stunt performer based in Amsterdam. With a deep passion for storytelling and an adventurous spirit, I bring authenticity and energy to every project I take on, whether it’s in front of the camera, on stage, or exploring physical challenges through stunts.

My journey in the arts began early, from school plays to professional productions. Over the years, I’ve honed my craft through diverse training programs, including acting schools in the Netherlands and an exchange year in the U.S. My portfolio spans everything from dramatic indie films to immersive theater experiences like The Amsterdam Dungeon. Each project has sharpened my skills and strengthened my commitment to delivering compelling performances.

Beyond acting, I’m an all-round creative with a love for physical storytelling. My athletic skills in snowboarding, parkour, kickboxing, and stage combat enable me to push boundaries and immerse myself fully in action-packed roles. Off set, I’ve also explored the modeling world, lending my style to campaigns for brands like ABN Amro and RegioBank.

When I’m not acting, I’m probably seeking inspiration through travel. From backpacking across Southeast Asia to solo interrailing through Norway, these experiences fuel my creativity and expand my understanding of the world – perspectives I bring to every character I portray.

I’m driven, versatile, and ready to take on challenging roles that connect with audiences. Whether you’re looking for a leading actor, a stunt performer, or someone who’s simply eager to collaborate, I’d love to help bring your vision to life.

Let’s create something extraordinary together!
